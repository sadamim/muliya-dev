Muliya – Gold & Diamonds

Muliya is a premium jewellery brand known for its fusion of heritage and innovation. With 5 showrooms of gold jewellery and 2 showrooms of Silveriya across Karnataka—including the region’s largest jewellery showroom in Puttur—we offer curated collections of gold, diamond, silver, and luxury watches.

Our customer-first approach, certified purity, and cutting-edge in-store technology make us a brand that evolves while staying true to its roots.

Our story begins in 1944

Founded by Late Shri Muliya Keshava Bhatta, Muliya has grown from a humble jewellery store in Puttur to a name trusted across Karnataka for over 81 years. Built on values of integrity, transparency, and deep customer connection, our story continues with the same spark that began it all.
